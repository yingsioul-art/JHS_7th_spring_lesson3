## C. 文法重點 A：序數與日期 (Ordinal Numbers & Dates)

> **核心句型**：What's the date today? / What's the date of Amy's birthday? → It's (on) April tenth / 10.

**例句**：
1. Q: What's the date today? A: It's January fifth / 5.
2. Q: What's the date today? A: It's May twelfth / 12.
3. Q: What is the date of your birthday? A: 請依實際狀況回答。

**日期 3 種表示法**：
- (1) 月份 + 序數：August eighth
- (2) 月份 + 阿拉伯數字：September 12
- (3) 月份 + 阿拉伯數字 + 序數字尾：December 31st

## C2. 文法重點 B：When 問句 (When Questions)

> **核心句型**：When is + 事件/節日? → It's on + 月份 + 日期.

**例句**：
1. When is Double Tenth Day? → It's on October tenth / 10.
2. When is the show? → It's on February thirteenth / 13.
3. When is the potluck? → It's on October thirty-first / 31.
4. When is the basketball game? → It's on March twentieth / 20.
5. When is the picnic? → It's on December fifteenth / 15.

**When vs What date / What day**：
- When → 萬用問時間
- What date → 限定回答日期
- What day → 限定回答星期幾

**時間介系詞 in / on / at**：

| 介系詞 | 搭配對象 | 範例 |
|--------|---------|------|
| in | 年份 / 月份 / 季節 / 早上下午晚上 | in 2020 / in May / in spring / in the morning |
| on | 日期 / 節日 / 星期幾 / 週末 | on July first / on Mother's Day / on Monday / on the weekend |
| at | 時刻 / 中午晚上 | at eleven o'clock / at night |

# 第三部分：📄 課文 (Reading) 解析 ⭐ 主題式閱讀理解

## G1. 課文主題與背景

**Pre-reading Question**: How do you and your family celebrate Chinese New Year?

本課課文以「世界各國新年慶祝方式」為主題，介紹三個國家（**India / Romania / Thailand**）並歸納共通點：標誌新的開始 + 為所有人帶來歡樂。

## G2. 逐段解析（4 段）

### 第 1 段：全世界慶祝新年

> The world celebrates the start of a new year in different ways. In India, for example, people throw colorful powders at each other. How do other countries celebrate it?

**中**：全世界以不同的方式慶祝新年的開始。例如，在印度，人們互相投擲彩色粉末。其他國家如何慶祝呢？

**關鍵字**：celebrate / for example / each other

**解析**：
- celebrate (v.) 慶祝，名詞形 celebration
- for example 用來舉例
- throw...at each other 互相投擲
- How do other countries celebrate it? — 用 How 問方式

**Fill 克漏**：The world ___ the start of a new year in different ways. In India, ___ ___, people throw colorful powders at ___ ___. → celebrates / for example / each other

### 第 2 段：羅馬尼亞的熊舞

> In Romania, people wear large bear costumes and dance for good luck on December 31st and January 1st. The dancing bears knock on doors, visit people's houses, and wish everyone a happy New Year.

**中**：在羅馬尼亞，人們穿著大型熊裝在十二月三十一日和一月一日跳舞求好運。跳舞的熊會敲門、拜訪人們的家，並祝每個人新年快樂。

**關鍵字**：large / costume / luck / 1st / knock / wish

**解析**：wear large bear costumes / for good luck / knock on doors / wish everyone a happy New Year（wish + 人 + 祝福語）

### 第 3 段：泰國的潑水節

> In Thailand, people call their New Year's Day Songkran. It's on April 13th. Songkran is a water festival. People have big water fights. For them, water can wash away bad luck.

**中**：在泰國，人們稱他們的新年為潑水節。在四月十三日。潑水節是一個水的節日。人們進行大規模的水仗。對他們而言，水可以洗去厄運。

**關鍵字**：call / wash

**解析**：call A B = 稱 A 為 B / It's on April 13th 日期前用 on / water fights / wash away bad luck

### 第 4 段：慶祝的共通點

> New Year celebrations are different from country to country, but they have two things in common. They mark a new start and bring joy for all.

**中**：各國的新年慶祝活動各不相同，但它們有兩個共同點。它們標誌著新的開始，並為所有人帶來歡樂。

**關鍵字**：celebration / thing / in common / mark / start

**解析**：be different from country to country / have...in common / mark a new start / bring joy for all

## G3. 閱讀後可討論的話題（5 條 — 與母語人士交流的話題種子）

1. How do you and your family celebrate Chinese New Year? What special foods do you eat?
2. If you could create a new festival for Taiwan, what would it celebrate, when would it be, and how would people enjoy it?
3. Of the three countries in the reading (India, Romania, Thailand), which New Year custom would you most like to try, and why?
4. What do you think 'mark a new start' and 'bring joy for all' really mean? Can you give examples from your own life?
5. Some festivals get loud and messy (Holi, Songkran). Do you think that's a good thing for a celebration? Why or why not?
